About

The earth produces hydrogen.
We made it investable.

Natural hydrogen — sometimes called white hydrogen — is generated continuously deep underground. It's carbon-neutral, ultra-low cost, and virtually limitless.

Why HYDROMA?

HYDROMA Inc. is the global pioneer in natural hydrogen production, operating the world's first commercial extraction site in Mali with wells yielding 98%+ purity hydrogen.

The company holds exploration rights across 43,174 km² and is expanding into Canada, Mauritania, Senegal, and Niger — each new market a potential re-rating catalyst.

Why tokenize?

Tokenization via the Brickken protocol enables fractional access to HYDROMA equity through a regulated UK SPV (Krechendo Managers Ltd), with full KYC/AML compliance and CertiK-audited smart contracts on BNB Chain.
